Experience

Pricing and Terms

I will travel to the student's home for lessons. I have a base fee of $40 per 30 min lesson if within a 10 min drive from Old Torrance. I will determine the drive time based on whatever google maps says for the time of day for our agreed meeting time, and it will be fixed from there.

I will change an additional $1.00 per minute for every minute after 10. For example, if you live 15 minutes away and want a 30 minute lesson... $40 + $5 = $45.00.

I accept Venmo or cash for all transactions. Pay in packages or one at a time. Whatever works best for you!

I will do my best to give 24 hours notice for any cancellations, and I expect students to do the same. I do not charge cancellation fees.

Any other questions, I'm excited to hear from you!
